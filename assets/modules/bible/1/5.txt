<h3 align='center'>Genesis 5</h3><ol id=loadedChapter><li>This is the book of the generations of Adam. In the day that God created man, in the likeness of God made he him;</li><li>Male and female created he them; and blessed them, and called their name Adam, in the day when they were created.</li><li>And Adam lived an hundred and thirty years, and begat a son in his own likeness, after his image; and called his name Seth:</li><li>And the days of Adam after he had begotten Seth were eight hundred years: and he begat sons and daughters:</li><li>And all the days that Adam lived were nine hundred and thirty years: and he died.</li><li>And Seth lived an hundred and five years, and begat Enos:</li><li>And Seth lived after he begat Enos eight hundred and seven years, and begat sons and daughters:</li><li>And all the days of Seth were nine hundred and twelve years: and he died.</li><li>And Enos lived ninety years, and begat Cainan:</li><li>And Enos lived after he begat Cainan eight hundred and fifteen years, and begat sons and daughters:</li><li>And all the days of Enos were nine hundred and five years: and he died.</li><li>And Cainan lived seventy years, and begat Mahalaleel:</li><li>And Cainan lived after he begat Mahalaleel eight hundred and forty years, and begat sons and daughters:</li><li>And all the days of Cainan were nine hundred and ten years: and he died.</li><li>And Mahalaleel lived sixty and five years, and begat Jared:</li><li>And Mahalaleel lived after he begat Jared eight hundred and thirty years, and begat sons and daughters:</li><li>And all the days of Mahalaleel were eight hundred ninety and five years: and he died.</li><li>And Jared lived an hundred sixty and two years, and he begat Enoch:</li><li>And Jared lived after he begat Enoch eight hundred years, and begat sons and daughters:</li><li>And all the days of Jared were nine hundred sixty and two years: and he died.</li><li>And Enoch lived sixty and five years, and begat Methuselah:</li><li>And Enoch walked with God after he begat Methuselah three hundred years, and begat sons and daughters:</li><li>And all the days of Enoch were three hundred sixty and five years:</li><li>And Enoch walked with God: and he was not; for God took him.</li><li>And Methuselah lived an hundred eighty and seven years, and begat Lamech:</li><li>And Methuselah lived after he begat Lamech seven hundred eighty and two years, and begat sons and daughters:</li><li>And all the days of Methuselah were nine hundred sixty and nine years: and he died.</li><li>And Lamech lived an hundred eighty and two years, and begat a son:</li><li>And he called his name Noah, saying, This same shall comfort us concerning our work and toil of our hands, because of the ground which the LORD hath cursed.</li><li>And Lamech lived after he begat Noah five hundred ninety and five years, and begat sons and daughters:</li><li>And all the days of Lamech were seven hundred seventy and seven years: and he died.</li><li>And Noah was five hundred years old: and Noah begat Shem, Ham, and Japheth.</li></ol>
